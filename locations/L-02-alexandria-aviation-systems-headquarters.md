# L-02 — Alexandria Aviation Systems Headquarters

## Owner / Responsible Organization
Alexandria Aviation Systems

## Accessibility
Controlled corporate access. Investigators receive broad access through executive authorization, subject to safety and data-handling requirements.

## Operational Status
Fully operational during the investigation, with portions of the company redirected toward anomaly analysis and intrusion response.

## Overview
Alexandria headquarters introduces the technology that has unintentionally expanded human perception. The facility embodies scientific integrity, disciplined engineering, and institutional transparency rather than corporate secrecy.

## Operational Function
L-02 is the primary investigative environment for understanding the sensor-fusion platform, validating its behavior, interviewing Alexandria personnel, and reconstructing the Collector intrusion.

## Physical Description
A modern research and engineering campus combining executive offices, collaborative workspaces, laboratories, secure data infrastructure, conference rooms, and facilities operations. The design is functional and professional rather than futuristic.

## Environmental Characteristics
Whiteboards, technical diagrams, archived datasets, testing equipment, and prototype hardware are visible throughout relevant work areas. Employees challenge assumptions openly and record failed explanations alongside successful findings. Security is substantial but appropriate to aviation technology and sensitive research.

- Secure research areas

## Editorial Theme
**Discovery**

Human ingenuity extends perception beyond ordinary biological limits. The investigators move from asking whether something happened to asking what Alexandria built that allows humanity to perceive it.

## GM Guidance
Never portray Alexandria as an antagonist by default. The operation depends upon investigators recognizing the company's credibility, transparency, and genuine commitment to safety.

Technical detail should communicate rigor rather than complexity. The headquarters feels believable because its professionals are trying to prove themselves wrong.

## Operational Notes
The intrusion should not transform Alexandria into a generic action location. Even afterward, Ortiz and the staff restore order through documentation, access control, and continuity procedures.
