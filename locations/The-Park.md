# The Park

**LOCKED CANONICAL**

The Park

I think this should remain hidden until the investigators build it themselves.

The FBI shouldn't know.

The families shouldn't know.

Nobody ever asked the question.

Only after interviewing multiple witnesses and comparing timelines do the investigators realize:

Every victim had independent reasons to spend time in the same wilderness area.

That's a classic Delta Green revelation.

A Real Location

I would strongly favor using a real New York state forest rather than inventing one.

Of the nearby public lands, I think Hogsback State Forest is particularly well suited. It's a real, roughly 1,100-acre state forest in Oneida County with unpaved forest roads, primitive recreation, and very little developed infrastructure—exactly the sort of place where different people could independently spend time without crossing paths.

It naturally supports all of your victim activities:

Samantha — hiking and wildlife photography.
Christopher — inspecting county emergency communications equipment or emergency planning sites.
Nate — hiking while testing SDR, GPS, or mesh networking equipment.
Hal — attempting to triangulate the persistent radio interference.
Dave — servicing HVAC equipment at a county maintenance building or communications facility.

Every visit is completely mundane.

The Pattern the Players Discover

One thing I love is that no single clue reveals the park.

Instead, every interview contributes one tiny piece.

For example:

Jennifer:

"Chris had to inspect a communications site every few months."

Rebecca:

"Dad kept disappearing into the woods trying to find that interference."

The Carters:

"Sam loved photographing birds up there."

Melissa:

"Dave mentioned having to repair an air conditioner at one of the county buildings."

The FBI:

Nate's impossible travel begins after entering the forest.

Only after putting all of those together does someone at the table say:

"Wait..."

"They all went to the same place."

That's exactly the kind of "earned revelation" we want.
