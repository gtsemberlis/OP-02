# H-01 — Initial Program Briefing

## Artifact Type
Operational briefing document

## Current Custody
Program archive; issued copy held by the investigators for the duration of the operation.

## Current Location
L-05 — Program Secure Briefing Facility

## First Appearance
S-01 — Initial Assignment

## Description
A concise Program briefing establishing the O'Hare hard landing, the impossible multi-modal obstruction report, the investigators' cover relationship to the official aviation investigation, and the operation's initial objectives.

## Presentation Notes
Present as a restrained federal briefing packet. Avoid faux-classified graphics, redactions used only for atmosphere, or language implying that the Program already understands the phenomenon.

## Investigator Access Conditions
Provided automatically by Michael Havel at assignment.

## Canonical Content
The briefing establishes the following:

- A commercial aircraft executed a hard landing at O'Hare after reporting a runway obstruction.
- Alexandria's safety platform independently generated the same warning.
- No conventional obstruction was recovered.
- Similar anomalies may exist in other records.
- The investigators are attached as authorized federal liaisons.
- Their objectives are to verify the anomaly, assess threat, evaluate Alexandria's continued operation, and recommend containment or mitigation.

The briefing does not identify the Collectors, the phenomenon's biological character, or any prior Program conclusion.

## Operational Significance
H-01 establishes the investigators' authority and the Program's reactive posture. It should frame the operation without pre-solving it.

## Preservation Notes
The issued copy may be retained as part of the operational file. Any investigator annotations become part of the campaign record.
