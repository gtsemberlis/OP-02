# A-02 — O'Hare Incident Summary

## Artifact Type
Official aviation incident summary

## Current Custody
NTSB original; Program archival copy.

## Current Location
L-04 — NTSB Field Office; Program copy at L-05.

## First Appearance
S-01 — Cold Open: O'Hare Hard Landing

## Description
A preliminary summary of the hard landing, casualty count, aircraft condition, air traffic control sequence, runway warning, and absence of a conventional obstruction.

## Presentation Notes
Format like an early factual report rather than a completed accident finding. Separate established facts from unresolved questions.

## Investigator Access Conditions
Available through the investigators' liaison authority.

## Canonical Content
The report records:

- Date, time, flight identification, aircraft type, and runway assignment.
- A runway obstruction warning during final approach.
- Independent corroboration from the Alexandria platform.
- Emergency maneuver and hard landing.
- Injuries and limited fatalities.
- No recovered debris, vehicle, animal remains, or runway incursion evidence.
- No immediate mechanical explanation.
- Continued evidence preservation and witness interviews.

## Operational Significance
A-02 gives investigators a stable factual baseline. Later testimony and technical evidence should be compared against it.
