# A-08 — Alexandria Runway Observation Record

## Artifact Type
Controlled experiment record

## Current Custody
Joint Alexandria and Program record; canonical Program copy.

## Current Location
Generated at L-01 — O'Hare controlled runway area; archived at L-05.

## First Appearance
S-08 — Runway Observation

## Description
The complete technical and observational record of the controlled runway experiment.

## Presentation Notes
Combine synchronized sensor plots, timestamps, environmental readings, observer statements, and a restrained event chronology.

## Investigator Access Conditions
Available to all authorized participants after the experiment.

## Canonical Content
The record documents:

- Rising confidence across independent sensor modalities.
- A large obstruction track crossing the runway environment.
- Bird avoidance before human perception.
- Brief, inconsistent human observation of a ray-like form.
- No aggressive response from the phenomenon.
- Continued sensor tracking after unaided perception ends.
- Differences between witness descriptions that remain broadly compatible.

## Operational Significance
A-08 is the strongest direct verification in the operation. It confirms that the phenomenon is real while preserving uncertainty about its full nature.

## Preservation Notes
Raw data, synchronized logs, and witness statements should be retained separately as well as within the consolidated record.
