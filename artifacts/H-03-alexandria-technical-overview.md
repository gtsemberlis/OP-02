# H-03 — Alexandria Technical Overview

## Artifact Type
Corporate technical briefing

## Current Custody
Alexandria Aviation Systems; Program copy authorized by Ethan Mercer.

## Current Location
L-02 — Alexandria Aviation Systems Headquarters

## First Appearance
S-02 — Alexandria Headquarters

## Description
A plain-language explanation of Alexandria's multi-modal aviation sensor-fusion platform, its validation model, confidence thresholds, and intended runway-safety function.

## Presentation Notes
Use clean technical diagrams and restrained corporate formatting. The handout should communicate rigor rather than futuristic spectacle.

## Investigator Access Conditions
Provided through Mercer's full cooperation.

## Canonical Content
The overview explains:

- Independent radar, lidar, optical, infrared, and environmental inputs.
- Validation of each stream before fusion.
- Confidence scoring and cross-modal corroboration.
- Suppression of single-sensor false positives.
- Retention of raw observations for later audit.
- The system's intended role as decision support rather than autonomous command.

## Operational Significance
H-03 establishes why the O'Hare warning is difficult to dismiss. The platform was designed specifically to prevent the type of false positive investigators initially suspect.

## Preservation Notes
The Program copy excludes unrelated proprietary data while retaining all information relevant to the operation.
