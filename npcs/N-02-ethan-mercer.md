# N-02 — Ethan Mercer

## Affiliation
Alexandria Aviation Systems

## Position / Occupation
Founder & Chief Executive Officer

## Primary Location
L-02 — Alexandria Aviation Systems Headquarters

## Current Status
Active and fully cooperative with the investigation.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Ethan James Mercer  
**Age:** 49  
**Education:** B.S., Aerospace Engineering — MIT; M.S., Systems Engineering — Georgia Tech  
**Previous Employment:** Boeing Phantom Works; FAA NextGen Research Program; founder of Alexandria Aviation Systems  
**Professional Specialties:** Aviation systems engineering, autonomous safety systems, multi-modal sensor integration, organizational leadership, aviation risk analysis  
**Professional Reputation:** A respected engineer-executive known for transparency with regulators and willingness to admit uncertainty.  
**Selected Projects:** FAA NextGen Sensor Integration Initiative; Alexandria Multi-Modal Runway Awareness Platform; Distributed Aviation Confidence Estimation Framework; Predictive Runway Incursion Prevention System  
**Recognition:** AIAA Outstanding Engineering Achievement Award; FAA Administrator's Industry Partnership Citation; Aviation Week Technology Leadership Recognition; IEEE Distinguished Systems Engineering Award  
**Residence:** Western suburbs of Chicago  
**Family:** Married; two teenage children.

## Physical Description
Mercer is a lean, athletic man in his late forties with dark hair graying at the temples. He favors practical, expensive but unostentatious business attire suitable for offices, laboratories, and airport facilities. He listens with complete attention and rarely dominates physical space. The first impression is an experienced engineer who happens to lead a company.

## Operational Role
Mercer authorizes unrestricted cooperation, establishes Alexandria's institutional posture, coordinates executive response during the intrusion, and accepts responsibility for the organization. He ensures experts can discover the truth.

## Biography
Mercer entered aerospace engineering to reduce preventable aviation accidents. He founded Alexandria as an aviation safety company that uses artificial intelligence, never as an AI company for its own sake. Alexandria's reputation for conservative engineering and rigorous validation is his greatest achievement.

## Private Context
Mercer fears institutional fear more than reputational damage. He believes Alexandria owes the public an honest accounting of reality, even when uncomfortable.

## Cognitive Signature
### Institutional Stewardship
His governing question is: “What allows this institution to continue doing good work tomorrow?” He treats ethics and organizational success as inseparable.

## Communication Style
Plain, direct, and free of executive affectation. He delegates technical questions to qualified specialists. His guiding statement is: “If our system is wrong, we need to know why. If it's right, we need to know that even more.”

## Under Pressure
Mercer becomes decisive, delegates broadly, and prioritizes employee safety, evidence preservation, continuity, and cooperation.

## Investigative Utility
Provides institutional perspective: Alexandria's mission, executive decisions, regulatory relationships, and long-term safety objectives.

## Relationships
**Dr. Rao:** Values her intellectual honesty and protects her freedom to ask difficult questions.  
**Kevin Park:** Trusts him to report engineering reality rather than convenience.  
**Javier Ortiz:** Relies on his operational judgment.  
**Michael Havel:** Trusts his professional conduct without requiring institutional transparency.

## Operational Knowledge
**Confirmed:** Alexandria's architecture, internal validation, deployment history, and response to the intrusion.  
**Suspects:** The intrusion initially represents industrial or state-sponsored espionage.  
**Unknown:** The phenomenon's nature, the Collectors' identity, and the Program's true institutional scope.

## Post-Operation Status
Mercer remains CEO. Alexandria's future relationship with the Program depends on the investigators' recommendation. He accepts oversight if it preserves safety and honest inquiry.

## Operational Notes
Never portray Mercer as a stereotypical corporate executive, profit-driven villain, or naïve idealist. He welcomes scrutiny and revises his beliefs when evidence requires it.

## Mechanical Reference
**Profession:** CEO / Aerospace Systems Engineer  
**Attributes:** High INT, POW, and CHA.  
**Key Skills:** Bureaucracy, Persuade, Accounting, Science (Engineering), Management, HUMINT, Law.  
**Combat:** Minimal.
