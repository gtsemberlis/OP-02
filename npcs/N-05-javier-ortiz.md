# N-05 — Javier Ortiz

## Affiliation
Alexandria Aviation Systems

## Position / Occupation
Director of Facilities & Security Operations

## Primary Location
L-02 — Alexandria Aviation Systems Headquarters

## Current Status
Active. Leads physical reconstruction and scene preservation following the intrusion.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Javier Alejandro Ortiz  
**Age:** 46  
**Education:** B.S., Industrial Engineering — University of Illinois Chicago; Certified Protection Professional; FEMA continuity certification  
**Previous Employment:** U.S. Air Force Civil Engineering Squadron; O'Hare operations; Alexandria Aviation Systems  
**Professional Specialties:** Physical security, facilities management, access control, continuity, emergency response  
**Professional Reputation:** Meticulous operations professional known for anticipating problems before they become emergencies.  
**Selected Projects:** Alexandria Headquarters Security Integration; Access Control Modernization; Secure Research Wing Expansion; Emergency Operations Continuity Program  
**Residence:** Northwest suburbs of Chicago  
**Family:** Married; three children; active in community youth athletics.

## Physical Description
Ortiz is broad-shouldered, solidly built, and in his mid-forties, with close-cropped dark hair graying at the temples and a neat beard. He moves through buildings with unconscious awareness of exits, cameras, and service spaces. The first impression is someone who immediately understands how a building works.

## Operational Role
Maintains continuity, coordinates emergency response, reconstructs the intrusion, and preserves physical evidence.

## Biography
Military infrastructure and airport operations taught Ortiz that successful operations depend on thousands of small systems functioning reliably. He considers invisibility the mark of good facilities work.

## Private Context
Ortiz believes buildings tell the truth. People misremember and systems fail, but physical movement leaves evidence.

## Cognitive Signature
### Operational Reconstruction
His governing question is: “What physically happened here?” He reconstructs pathways, timing, access, logistics, and constraints.

## Communication Style
Plain, efficient, and practical. He prefers “Let's go look” to abstract discussion.

## Under Pressure
Secures areas, establishes perimeters, coordinates personnel, documents changes, and preserves evidence.

## Investigative Utility
Provides physical and operational perspective, especially during S-07.

## Relationships
**Mercer:** Near-implicit trust.  
**Kevin:** Strong working relationship where infrastructure and systems intersect.  
**Rao:** Quietly watches over researchers' wellbeing.  
**Investigators:** Respects those who physically examine scenes.

## Operational Knowledge
**Confirmed:** Building layout, access logs, physical routes, security gaps, and intrusion sequence.  
**Suspects:** An unusually disciplined recovery team rather than ordinary criminals.  
**Unknown:** The Collectors' identity and the phenomenon's meaning.

## Post-Operation Status
Remains Alexandria's operations director. He becomes a reliable Program-adjacent contact if Alexandria continues under supervision.

## Operational Notes
Never reduce Ortiz to “the security guy.” He is an operations professional, grounded in physical evidence and uninterested in unsupported supernatural conclusions.

## Mechanical Reference
**Profession:** Facilities & Security Director  
**Attributes:** High CON and POW; above-average STR and INT.  
**Key Skills:** Alertness, Search, Bureaucracy, Criminology, Navigate, Heavy Machinery, Athletics, Drive, Persuade.  
**Combat:** Competent through prior military and emergency-response training.
