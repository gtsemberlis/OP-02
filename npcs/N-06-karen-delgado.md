# N-06 — Karen Delgado

## Affiliation
National Transportation Safety Board

## Position / Occupation
Senior Air Safety Investigator

## Primary Location
L-04 — NTSB Field Office

## Current Status
Active liaison for the O'Hare investigation.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Karen Michelle Delgado  
**Age:** 41  
**Education:** B.S., Aviation Management — Embry-Riddle; M.S., Human Factors & Aviation Safety — USC  
**Previous Employment:** Regional airline operations; FAA Aviation Safety Inspector; NTSB  
**Professional Specialties:** Accident investigation, witness interviewing, human factors, crew resource management, evidence preservation  
**Professional Reputation:** Thorough, calm, and unusually effective with both technical experts and ordinary witnesses.  
**Residence:** Chicago metropolitan area  
**Family:** Married; one daughter, age nine.

## Physical Description
Delgado is an athletic woman in her early forties with dark hair usually tied back, practical professional clothing, sturdy shoes, and an NTSB badge always visible. She carries a notebook but uses it sparingly during interviews. The first impression is someone who listens more carefully than she speaks.

## Operational Role
Coordinates witness access, explains accident-investigation procedure, and facilitates the interview sequence. She unknowingly preserves A-06.

## Biography
Delgado moved from airline operations to the FAA and then the NTSB because she wanted to understand why accidents occur. She developed a reputation for preserving memory without shaping it.

## Private Context
She believes every witness deserves patience and dignity. Fragmented traumatic memory is documented, not forced into false certainty.

## Cognitive Signature
### Factual Reconstruction
Her governing question is: “What do we know this person actually experienced?”

## Communication Style
Calm, warm, open-ended, and non-leading. “Tell me what you remember” is her natural prompt.

## Under Pressure
Becomes gentler, slows interviews, allows silence, and protects witness wellbeing.

## Investigative Utility
Provides NTSB methodology and creates the conditions for honest testimony. Owen's drawing remains incidental to her.

## Relationships
**Investigators:** Fellow professionals if they respect procedure.  
**FAA Personnel:** Cooperative while preserving NTSB independence.  
**Sarah Walker:** Builds trust through concern for Owen.  
**Owen Walker:** Gives him plain white office paper and crayons without asking him to draw the accident.

## Operational Knowledge
**Confirmed:** Accident chronology, witness accounts, human-factors evidence, and NTSB procedure.  
**Suspects:** An unusual but conventional aviation incident.  
**Unknown:** The phenomenon, the Program, the Collectors, and the significance of A-06.

## Post-Operation Status
Continues the official investigation and supports a conventional public report. She may remain a valuable professional contact.

## Operational Notes
Never portray Delgado as suspicious, obstructive, or secretly aware of the Unnatural. She treats A-06 as a child's drawing.

## Mechanical Reference
**Profession:** NTSB Air Safety Investigator  
**Attributes:** High INT and POW; above-average CHA.  
**Key Skills:** Bureaucracy, HUMINT, Persuade, Criminology, Alertness, Search, Navigate, First Aid, Law (Transportation).  
**Combat:** Minimal.
