# N-12 — Owen Walker

## Affiliation
Civilian

## Position / Occupation
Second-grade student

## Primary Location
L-01 — Chicago O'Hare International Airport

## Current Status
Surviving child witness.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Owen James Walker  
**Age:** 7  
**Residence:** Naperville, Illinois  
**Interests:** Drawing, dinosaurs, LEGO, airplanes, space; favorite color blue.

## Physical Description
A cheerful, small seven-year-old with untidy light brown hair, travel clothes, sneakers, and a nearby backpack. He fidgets while adults talk and becomes still when drawing.

## Operational Role
Unintentionally creates A-06, an unfiltered visual record of what he remembers.

## Biography
An ordinary, imaginative child who processes unfamiliar experiences by drawing. He is not unusually perceptive and has no special relationship to the Unnatural.

## Cognitive Signature
### Direct Observation
His governing question is: “What did I see?” He has not learned to classify observations as ordinary or impossible.

## Communication Style
Natural for a seven-year-old: brief, honest, distractible, and more comfortable with gentle conversation than formal questioning.

## Investigative Utility
Rewards investigators who notice ordinary human moments. He treats the manta-ray shapes exactly like the airplane and clouds.

## Operational Notes
Never portray Owen as psychic, specially sensitive, or an evidentiary instrument. He is never interviewed alone or interrogated. Sarah remains present.
