# N-09 — Lisa Morales

## Affiliation
Commercial Airline Cabin Crew

## Position / Occupation
Senior Flight Attendant

## Primary Location
L-01 — Chicago O'Hare International Airport

## Current Status
Surviving crew witness.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Lisa Anne Morales  
**Age:** 39  
**Experience:** Sixteen years; qualified on multiple aircraft; cabin safety instructor.

## Physical Description
An athletic woman in her late thirties whose uniform and movements reflect reassuring competence and constant situational awareness.

## Operational Role
Provides the professional cabin perspective: crew coordination, passenger behavior, safety procedure, and the human experience inside the aircraft.

## Biography
Morales understands that cabin work is fundamentally responsibility rather than hospitality. This is the first incident in which flawless procedure still fails to produce a familiar explanation.

## Cognitive Signature
### Human Observation
Her governing question is: “How are people responding?”

## Communication Style
Warm, chronological, and careful not to assign blame. She separates personal observation from later information.

## Investigative Utility
Confirms professional crew behavior and preserves the lived operational reality of the cabin.

## Operational Notes
Never portray Morales as “just the flight attendant” or as too shaken to be reliable. She is an experienced aviation safety professional.
