# N-07 — Mark Reynolds

## Affiliation
Civilian

## Position / Occupation
Regional Sales Director

## Primary Location
L-01 — Chicago O'Hare International Airport

## Current Status
Surviving witness.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Mark Andrew Reynolds  
**Age:** 52  
**Residence:** Minneapolis  
**Travel Profile:** Approximately 120–150 flights annually; decades of frequent travel.

## Physical Description
A fit man in his early fifties with graying brown hair, practical business attire, and well-used carry-on luggage. He appears completely at ease within the modern airline system.

## Operational Role
Provides long-term experiential context by comparing the incident against thousands of ordinary flights.

## Biography
Nearly three decades of constant business travel have given Reynolds an intuitive sense of crew behavior, turbulence, timing, and airport rhythm.

## Cognitive Signature
### Pattern Recognition
His governing question is: “Is this normal?”

## Communication Style
Conversational, chronological, and comparative. He often begins with “Usually…” or “I've flown through O'Hare hundreds of times…”

## Investigative Utility
Establishes that the event violated deeply familiar operational expectations without providing technical explanation.

## Operational Notes
He is not an aviation expert. His value is accumulated experience. His testimony reinforces other evidence rather than resolving the investigation.
