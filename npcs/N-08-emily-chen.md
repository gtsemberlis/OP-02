# N-08 — Emily Chen

## Affiliation
Civilian

## Position / Occupation
Senior Mechanical Engineer; aviation enthusiast

## Primary Location
L-01 — Chicago O'Hare International Airport

## Current Status
Surviving witness.

## Personnel Record
**Full Name:** Emily Mei Chen  
**Age:** 36  
**Residence:** Chicago  
**Interests:** Commercial aviation, aircraft photography, transportation systems, aviation history.

## Physical Description
An athletic woman in her mid-thirties with shoulder-length dark hair, practical clothing, and a compact camera. She watches ground crews, taxiways, and sequencing as closely as aircraft.

## Operational Role
Provides informed civilian technical and procedural observations without institutional authority.

## Biography
A mechanical engineer whose interest expanded from aircraft design to the coordination of aviation systems. She is knowledgeable but careful not to present herself as an aviation professional.

## Cognitive Signature
### Procedural Observation
Her governing question is: “Why would they do that?”

## Communication Style
Careful, enthusiastic, plain-language, and humble. She distinguishes knowledge from suspicion.

## Investigative Utility
Recognizes deviations in runway assignment, movement, sequencing, and procedure.

## Operational Notes
Never portray Chen as an amateur trying to impress investigators. Her expertise comes from sustained curiosity and intellectual humility.
