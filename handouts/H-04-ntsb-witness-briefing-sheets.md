# NATIONAL TRANSPORTATION SAFETY BOARD

## Witness Briefing Sheet

**Interview Number:** W-01
**Investigation:** ORD Runway Incident
**Interview Status:** Pending
**Prepared By:** Karen Delgado, Air Safety Investigator

---

## Witness Identification

**Name:** Daniel Price

**Age:** 46

**Occupation:** Regional Sales Director

**Residence:** Indianapolis, Indiana

---

## Relationship to Incident

Passenger aboard Midwest Air Flight 271.

Assigned Seat:

3A (Port side, forward cabin.)

---

## Selection Rationale

Mr. Price was selected for interview based upon:

* Forward seating position.
* Extensive commercial flight experience.
* Calm demeanor following the incident.
* Detailed spontaneous observations provided immediately after landing.

---

## Relevant Background

According to airline records, Mr. Price averages approximately 120–150 commercial flight segments annually.

He reports no aviation training.

He has previously participated in two routine FAA witness interviews following unrelated operational delays.

No history of incident exaggeration or formal complaints was identified during preliminary review.

---

## Medical Status

No reported physical injuries.

Evaluated by airport medical personnel.

Released without further treatment.

---

## Interview Objectives

Determine:

* Sequence of events during final approach.
* Passenger awareness of crew actions.
* Cabin atmosphere immediately before landing.
* Any observations regarding aircraft motion or unusual sounds.
* General situational awareness during the event.

Avoid technical interpretation.

Witness should describe observations only.

---

## Investigator Notes

Mr. Price appears comfortable discussing the incident.

Initial conversations suggest unusually precise chronological recall.

Allow uninterrupted narrative before asking clarifying questions.

Do not introduce technical terminology.

---

## Materials Available

* Passenger seating chart
* Cabin layout
* Flight timeline
* Airport approach diagram
