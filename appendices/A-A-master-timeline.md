# Appendix A — Master Timeline

## Purpose
Provide the Game Master with a single chronological view of Operation Airspace, separating the underlying history of the phenomenon from the investigators' experience.

## Historical Timeline

| Time | Event |
|---|---|
| Years prior | Naturally occurring aerial organisms exist unnoticed by humanity. |
| Prior months | Alexandria's sensor-fusion platform begins producing repeatable anomaly detections. |
| Following validation | Additional historical incidents are identified across multiple locations. |
| Before the operation | Dr. Ananya Rao privately documents recurring patterns. |
| Before the operation | The Collectors become aware of Rao's research. |

## Operational Timeline

| Scene | Event |
|---|---|
| S-01 | O'Hare hard landing; Program assigns investigators. |
| S-02 | Alexandria headquarters briefing and technical overview. |
| S-03 | Interview with Dr. Rao; research notebook introduced. |
| S-04 | Internal engineering investigation eliminates conventional explanations. |
| S-05 | NTSB witness interviews independently corroborate the anomaly. |
| S-06 | Collector intrusion; notebook fragments recovered. |
| S-07 | Controlled runway observation confirms the phenomenon. |
| S-08 | Program debrief and operational recommendation. |

## GM Use
Consult this appendix whenever reconstructing chronology, answering player questions about sequence, or validating continuity between scenes.

## Editorial Notes
This appendix is chronological only. It intentionally avoids interpretation or causation beyond what the operation establishes.
