# S-07 — The Collectors

## Chapter

Chapter 4 — Competing Observers

## Scene Summary

Rao contacts the investigators while hiding from unknown intruders seeking her notebooks. The confrontation reveals that another organization has prior knowledge of the phenomenon.

## Scene Purpose

Introduce external pressure, force immediate action, and establish historical awareness without resolving the Collectors' identity.

## Primary Location(s)

L-03 — Dr. Rao's Office or residence; L-02 if the intrusion occurs at Alexandria

## Participants

N-03 Dr. Ananya Rao; N-05 Javier Ortiz; N-13 The Collectors

## Estimated Runtime

30–60 minutes

## Investigation Phase

Escalation

## Scene Flow

1. Rao makes a frightened but coherent call.
2. Investigators arrive as two or three Collectors withdraw.
3. A brief confrontation or pursuit occurs; one Collector may die.
4. The body and recovered fragments reveal repaired gear, no electronics, and field notes about similar observations.

## Key Information

- The Collectors expected handwritten material and avoided electronic equipment.
- Their clothing and boots are maintained rather than disposable.
- Their notebook contains diagrams and symbols consistent with long-term field observation.
- Nothing recovered identifies a clear sponsor.

## Evidence Available

A-07 — Collector Notebook Fragments

## Likely Investigator Approaches

Rapid response, tactical movement, pursuit, forensic search, medical aid, and protection of Rao.

## GM Guidance

Keep combat short and consequential. The scene's purpose is evidence and pressure, not defeating the organization.

## Before Leaving This Scene

Rao should be safe or in custody, the notebook fragments secured, and the investigators aware they are not the first observers.

## Quick Reference

Critical details: no electronics, repaired equipment, handwritten historical record.
