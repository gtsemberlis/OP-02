# Editorial Philosophy

## Section Purpose

Canonical editorial philosophy for OP-01.

## Canonical Content

### Why This Book Exists

*Operation Airspace* is written as an operational reference rather than a traditional roleplaying adventure.

Its purpose is not to script events or anticipate every investigator decision. It provides the Game Master with a coherent understanding of the operation so investigator choices can be adjudicated naturally while preserving internal consistency, pacing, and themes.

The objective is not to guide investigators toward a predetermined solution.

The objective is to present a coherent reality that responds honestly to investigation.

### Investigative Philosophy

**Reality is assembled from many incomplete but honest observations.**

No single NPC possesses every answer. No single clue resolves the investigation. Investigators construct understanding by comparing independent perspectives, physical evidence, institutional knowledge, and their own judgment.

Evidence reinforces evidence. Observations corroborate observations. The investigation rewards attention rather than mechanical clue collection.

### Portraying NPCs

Every NPC is written as a person first and a narrative function second. Motivations, limitations, and decisions remain consistent even when investigators behave unexpectedly.

NPCs do not exist solely to deliver exposition or redirect the investigation. If an NPC does not know something, they say so. If an NPC changes their mind, new evidence warrants the change.

### Running the Investigation

Scenes describe likely operational situations, not mandatory sequences. When investigators act unexpectedly, rely on documented motivations, established facts, and campaign principles rather than preserving a preferred route.

### The Role of the Game Master

The Game Master is not responsible for protecting the story.

The Game Master is responsible for protecting the integrity of the world.

Run the people honestly. Present the evidence faithfully. Allow the investigators to earn their conclusions.
